San Roque Church
B. Morcilla St. Bgy. Poblacion, Pateros, Metro Manila Built in 1815 having San Roque as the patron. It has a high tower on the right side where the church bell is situated. The interior of the church is well-furnished with new benches, chandeliers, and a well-planned simple altar. Its architecture is simple both inside and outside.
